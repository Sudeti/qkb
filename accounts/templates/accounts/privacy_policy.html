PRIVACY POLICY & GDPR

1. DATA COLLECTION

We collect the following personal data when you create an account:

  • Email address (required for account verification)
  • Username (required for login)
  • Password (stored as encrypted hash, never in plain text)
  • Profile information (optional): first name, last name, phone, bio, avatar
  • IP addresses (for security and verification logs, anonymized after 1 year)
  • Account activity: date joined, last login, email verification status

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To provide and maintain your account
  • To verify your email address and prevent fraud
  • To send important account-related communications
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: View all your personal data in your dashboard
  • Right to Rectification: Update your profile information at any time
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in JSON format
  • Right to Object: Withdraw consent for data processing

You can exercise these rights directly from your dashboard.

4. DATA RETENTION

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Email verification logs: Automatically deleted after 1 year
  • IP addresses: Anonymized (only first 2 octets retained) after 1 year
  • Deleted accounts: All data is permanently removed within 30 days

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure session management
  • Regular security audits

6. COOKIES

We use cookies for:

  • Session management (required for login)
  • Analytics (with your consent)

You can manage cookie preferences through the cookie consent banner.

7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth (for social login) - subject to Google's privacy policy
  • Email service provider (for sending verification emails)

8. CONTACT

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your GDPR rights, please contact us at:

info@qkb.al

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
